Tehillim

There are many ways to read Tehillim. Some try to read the entire book every day for spiritual benefit. Another custom is to recite the Tehillim of your age plus one (Eg. If you are 20 years old, you would recite Tehillim 21). Others prefer to read whichever speaks to them. Among the most famous are Tehillim 1, Tehillim 121, and Tehillim 23.
