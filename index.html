Psalm 23

Learn to read, pronounce, and understand Psalm 23 in Hebrew.

יְהוָה רֹעִי לֹא אֶחְסָר
Adonai ro’i lo echsar
The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want.
בִּנְאוֹת דֶּשֶׁא יַרְבִּיצֵנִי
Bin’ot deshe yarbitzeni
He makes me lie down in green pastures.
עַל מֵי מְנֻחוֹת יְנַהֲלֵנִי
Al mei menuchot yenahaleini
He leads me beside still waters.
נַפְשִׁי יְשׁוֹבֵב
Nafshi yeshovev
He restores my soul.
יַנְחֵנִי בְמַעְגְּלֵי צֶדֶק לְמַעַן שְׁמוֹ
Yancheini b’ma’aglei tzedek lema’an shmo
He leads me in paths of righteousness for His name’s sake.
